9600 baud
fletcher16 checksum
most significant byte on left
2 byte confirm messages in response to certian messages; checksum of full message.
ETB - end of transmission block - dec. 17, 0x17
US - unit seperator - dec. 31, 0x1F

0x0* - 7  byte: tag, 32 bit fixed point, hash
0x1* - 12 byte: tag, lat (32 bit), lon (32 bit), order, hash
0x2* - 3  byte: tag, hash
0x30 - 4  byte: confirmation hash, hash
<tag> <a><b><c><d> <check><sum> <ETB><US>
<tag> <l><a><t><t> <l><o><n><g> <order> <check><sum> <ETB><US>
<tag> <action> <check><sum> <ETB><US>

0x00 - latitude
0x01 - longitude
0x02 - heading
0x03 - pitch
0x04 - roll
0x05 - speed
0x06 - distance

0x10 - add waypoint
0x11 - change waypoint
0x12 - delete waypoint
0x13 - rover at waypoint
0x14 - send rover to waypoint

0x21 - loop waypoints on
0x22 - loop waypoints off
0x23 - clear waypoint list
